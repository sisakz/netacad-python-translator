<h2>Scenario</h2>

<p>The Beatles were one of the most popular music groups of the 1960s, and the best-selling band in history. Some people consider them to be the most influential act of the rock era. Indeed, they were included in <i>Time</i> magazine's compilation of the 20th Century's 100 most influential people.</p>

<p>The band underwent many line-up changes, culminating in 1962 with the line-up of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Richard Starkey (better known as Ringo Starr).</p>

<br>

<p>Write a program that reflects these changes and lets you practice with the concept of lists. Your task is to:</p>

<ul>

<li>step 1: create an empty list named <codel>beatles</codel>;</li>

<li>step 2: use the <codel>append()</codel> method to add the following members of the band to the list: <codel>John Lennon</codel>, <codel>Paul McCartney</codel>, and <codel>George Harrison</codel>;</li>

<li>step 3: use the <codel>for</codel> loop and the <codel>append()</codel> method to prompt the user to add the following members of the band to the list: <codel>Stu Sutcliffe</codel>, and <codel>Pete Best</codel>;</li>

<li>step 4: use the <codel>del</codel> instruction to remove <codel>Stu Sutcliffe</codel> and <codel>Pete Best</codel> from the list;</li>

<li>step 5: use the <codel>insert()</codel> method to add <codel>Ringo Starr</codel> to the beginning of the list.</li>

</ul>

<p>By the way, are you a Beatles fan? (The Beatles is one of Greg's favorite bands. But wait...who's Greg...?)</p>
