<h1>Operators and their bindings: exponentiation</h1>



<p>Repeat the experiment, but now with exponentiation.</p>

<p>Use this snippet of code:</p>

<codep class="syntax-color">print(2 ** 2 ** 3)
</codep>
 
<br>

<p>The two possible results are:</p>

<ul>
<li><codel>2 ** 2</codel> &rarr; <codel>4</codel>; <codel>4 ** 3</codel> &rarr; <codel>64</codel></li>
<li><codel>2 ** 3</codel> &rarr; <codel>8</codel>; <codel>2 ** 8</codel> &rarr; <codel>256</codel></li>
</ul>

<br>

<p>Run the code. What do you see?</p>

<p>The result clearly shows that <strong>the exponentiation operator uses right-sided binding</strong>.</p>
