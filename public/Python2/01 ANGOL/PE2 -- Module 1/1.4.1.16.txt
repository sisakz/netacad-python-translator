<h1>How to use <i>pip</i>: continued</h1>


<p>If any of the currently installed packages are <b>no longer needed</b> and you want to get rid of them, <i>pip</i> will be useful, too. Its <codel>uninstall</codel> command will execute all the needed steps. </p>

<p>The required syntax is clear and simple:</p>

<p><codep class="syntax-color">pip uninstall package_name
</codep></p>

<p>so if you don't want <i>pygame</i> anymore, you can execute the following command:</p>

<p><codep class="syntax-color">pip uninstall pygame
</codep></p>

<p><i>Pip</i> will want to know if you’re sure about the choice you're making – be prepared to give the right answer.</p>
