<p>The question that should be put now is: how to get a proper cheese knife? In other words, how to ensure that <i>pip</i> is installed and ready to work?</p>

<p>The most precise answer is “it depends”. Really.</p>

<p>Some Python installations come with <i>pip</i>, some don't. What’s more, it doesn’t only depend on the OS you use, although this is a very important factor.</p>
