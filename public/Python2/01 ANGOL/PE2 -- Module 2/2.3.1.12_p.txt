<h1>The <font face="Courier New">rfind()</font> method</h1>

<p>The one-, two-, and three-parameter versions of the <codel>rfind()</codel> method do nearly the same things as their counterparts (the ones devoid of the <i>r</i> prefix), but <strong>start their searches from the end of the string</strong>, not the beginning (hence the prefix <i>r</i>, for <i>right</i>).</p>
