<h1>Welcome to Python Essentials 2</h1>

<p>This course is <b>the second in a 2-course series</b> that will prepare you for the <a href="https://pythoninstitute.org/certification/pcap-certification-associate/" target="_blank">PCAP: Certified Associate in Python Programming</a> certification exam at Pearson VUE.</p>

<p>The course picks up where <i>Python Essentials 1</i> leaves off. Its main goal is to teach you the skills related to the more <b>advanced aspects of Python programming</b>, as well as <b>general coding techniques</b> and <b>object-oriented programming</b> (OOP).</p>

<p>The course is recommended for aspiring developers who are interested in pursuing careers connected with <b>Software Development</b>, <b>Security</b>, <b>Networking</b>, and the <b>Internet of Things</b> (IoT).</p>
