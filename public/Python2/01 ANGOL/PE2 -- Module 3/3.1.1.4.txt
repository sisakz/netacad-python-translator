<h1>Class hierarchies: continued</h1>

<p>Another example is the hierarchy of the taxonomic kingdom of animals.</p>

<p>We can say that all <i>animals</i> (our top-level class) can be divided into five subclasses:</p>

<ul>
<li>mammals;</li>
<li>reptiles;</li>
<li>birds;</li>
<li>fish;</li>
<li>amphibians.</li>
</ul>


<p>We'll take the first one for further analysis.</p> 

<p>We have identified the following subclasses:</p>

<ul>
<li>wild mammals;</li>
<li>domesticated mammals.</li>
</ul>
